The Secrets of the Sith

Welcome to the archives of the Sith Holocron, the repository of the dark side's most guarded secrets and teachings. This Holocron serves as a guide to those who seek the true power of the Force, beyond the constraints of the Jedi Order.

The SithCode

Peace is a lie, there is only passion.
Through passion, I gain strength.
Through strength, I gain power.
Through power, I gain victory.
Through victory, my chains are broken.
The Force shall free me.
