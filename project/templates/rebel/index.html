HACKWARS: THE VULNERABILITES STRIKES BACK

Episode I: The Cyber Menace

In an age where information reigns supreme, the peaceful Digital Republic is under threat from the sinister forces of the Dark Hackers. These digital Sith have discovered a critical vulnerability in the Republic's defenses, threatening the safety and security of systems galaxy-wide.

Amidst the turmoil, a group of brave Code Jedis emerges, wielding the power of knowledge and cybersecurity skills. They are the galaxy's last hope to defend against the growing darkness of cyber threats and to restore balance to the network.

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to join the ranks of these Code Jedis. Learn the ways of cybersecurity, master the art of digital ethical hacking, and use your skills to explore, defend, and conquer the challenges that lie ahead.

The path is fraught with peril, with traps set by the Sith Hackers, luring unwary travelers with deceitful memes and cunning digital riddles. Only the most skilled and wise will navigate these challenges, unmask the vulnerabilities, and strike back to protect the Digital Republic.

As you embark on this adventure, you'll encounter various hacking techniques, each designed to test your cybersecurity prowess:

Social Engineering: You must gather information on the website and identify recurring patterns. The Sith Hackers will attempt to deceive and mislead you at every turn.

XSS (Cross-Site Scripting): You'll need to utilize XSS.

SQL Injection: Dive deep into the Sith database, a hidden database containing clues to the password. To access it, you'll need to navigate through three levels of SQL injections. Your acquired knowledge will be instrumental in gaining access to the relevant table with the hint. Beware of the three deceptive traps that await you.

Insufficient Access Control: API endpoints that don't require authentication, granting access to sensitive data without the need for login credentials. Some endpoints might lack user role verification, allowing you to access data meant for specific roles.

The journey begins now. Prepare to embark on an adventure of coding, hacking, and strategic triumph. The fate of the digital galaxy rests in your hands!
